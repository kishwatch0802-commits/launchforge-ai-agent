# Growth Funnel Designer

## Purpose
Turn launch channels into a stage-by-stage conversion model.

## When To Use
Use for marketing planning, bottleneck detection, and Copilot funnel questions.

## Inputs
Business type, launch channels, personas, and offer ladder.

## Outputs
Funnel stages, starting volume, conversion rate, output volume, bottleneck flag, and improvement recommendation.

## Constraints
Use planning assumptions only. Keep funnel stages specific to the business type.

## Safety Notes
Avoid inventing external traffic data.
