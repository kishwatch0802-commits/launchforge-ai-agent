# Launch Pack Validator

## Purpose
Validate that a LaunchForge pack is complete, business-type-specific, exportable, and honest about assumptions.

## When To Use
Use before export, video demos, or any handoff to the founder.

## Inputs
Business input, classification, readiness score, technical artefacts, roadmap, risks, assumptions, and trace.

## Outputs
Validation notes, missing sections, and go/no-go readiness for export.

## Constraints
Do not inflate readiness. Do not remove financial disclaimers. Do not persist private founder input.

## Safety Notes
Flag missing validation evidence and avoid presenting forecasts as guaranteed.
